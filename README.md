# Village

A local social network for apartment communities. Village helps residents connect with the people who live around them — fostering the kind of neighborly awareness that most apartment complexes lack despite high physical density.

## Why

Apartment living puts hundreds of people within feet of each other, yet most residents couldn't name a single neighbor. Village closes that gap by giving each building its own private, resident-only space to share, ask, and connect.

## Village Square (v0)

The first release is **Village Square** — a shared bulletin board for your building.

- **Resident verification** — invite codes distributed in-building (lobby flyers, property manager) keep the space resident-only
- **Postings** — create and browse posts scoped to your building
- **Comments + notifications** — discuss postings and get notified about activity you're subscribed to
